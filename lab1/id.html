Olaf's Favorite Seasons

Meet Olaf, the sun-loving snowman, and discover his favorite seasons!

Winter Wonderland

Olaf enjoys the magical winter wonderland, where everything is covered in a blanket of snow. He loves building snowmen and having snowball fights with his friends.

Fun in the Sun

But Olaf's heart truly belongs to summer! He dreams of warm beaches, sunshine, and enjoying the company of friends under the clear blue sky.
